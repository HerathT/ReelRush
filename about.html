About ReelRush

ReelRush is a blazing-fast and secure movie and TV show discovery platform powered by TMDb. Inspired by yts.ag, we deliver a simple yet elegant experience that focuses on what matters — finding and downloading your favorite titles quickly.

Our Mission

To empower movie lovers worldwide with access to a fast, clean, and intuitive interface for exploring cinema. ReelRush connects you to the data you need without distractions.

Technology Stack

  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla)
  • TMDb API Integration
  • Fast-loading secure architecture
  • Responsive design with full-screen layouts

Credits

All movie data and posters are provided by The Movie Database (TMDb).
